CATEGORY: FINANCIAL & BANKING SCAMS (HIGH RISK)

SCENARIO: FAKE ACCOUNT FREEZE (Classic Phishing)
Input Type: SMS / Short Text
Content: "Banca Transilvania: Accesul dvs. a fost restrictionat. Pentru deblocare, accesati urgent: http://bit.ly/bt-secure-login-ro. Aveti 24h la dispozitie."
Why Scam: Urgency ("urgent", "24h"), Shortened link (bit.ly), Threat of restriction.

SCENARIO: FAKE UPDATE (Generic Bank)
Input Type: Email
From: "Support Team" <update-security-team@yahoo.com>
Subject: Important Notification
Content: "Dear customer, our security system requires you to update your details. Failure to do so will result in account suspension."
Why Scam: Sender uses public domain (@yahoo.com) for official business. Banks NEVER do this.

SCENARIO: PAYPAL INVOICE FRAUD
Input Type: Email
From: "PayPal Billing" <billing-dept-paypal@gmail.com>
Content: "You have successfully paid $499.00 USD for Norton Antivirus. If you did not authorize this, call +1-800-FAKE-NUM immediately to cancel."
Why Scam: Fake sender (@gmail), Pressure to call a fake number (Vishing), High unexpected amount.

SCENARIO: ING FAKE LOGIN
Input Type: SMS
Content: "ING: Am detectat o conectare suspecta. Confirma identitatea aici: https://ing-homebank-security.net/login"
Why Scam: The domain 'ing-homebank-security.net' is a fake spoof. Official domain is 'ing.ro'.

SCENARIO: REVOLUT FROZEN ACCOUNT
Input Type: App Notification / SMS
Content: "Revolut: Your card is blocked due to unusual activity. Verify your ID: http://revolut-support-center.com"
Why Scam: Link points to a fake support domain, not the app.

CATEGORY: DELIVERY & LOGISTICS (HIGH RISK)

SCENARIO: FAN COURIER TAX (Smishing)
Input Type: SMS
Content: "FanCourier: Livrarea AW123456 a esuat. Adresa incompleta. Actualizati datele si achitati taxa de 4.99 RON aici: http://short.url/fan-delivery"
Why Scam: Request for small payment to steal card details. Official couriers don't ask for link payments via SMS for address updates.

SCENARIO: POSTA ROMANA VAMA
Input Type: SMS
Content: "Posta Romana: Pachetul dvs. este retinut la vama. Taxe vamale neachitate: 12 RON. Plateste acum: http://posta-taxe.online"
Why Scam: "posta-taxe.online" is a fake domain. Unexpected customs fee via SMS link.

SCENARIO: DHL EXPRESS FAKE
Input Type: Email
From: "DHL Tracking" <track-dhl-express@hotmail.com>
Content: "Your shipment is on hold. Click attached file to view shipping documents."
Why Scam: Sender uses @hotmail. Dangerous attachment (likely malware/virus).

CATEGORY: SOCIAL ENGINEERING & EMPLOYMENT (HIGH RISK)

SCENARIO: TIKTOK / YOUTUBE JOB SCAM
Input Type: WhatsApp / Telegram
Content: "Hello! We are hiring part-time staff. Earn 300-800 RON daily by liking YouTube videos. Payment is instant via USDT crypto. Contact manager on Telegram: @Work_Home_Boss"
Why Scam: "Too good to be true" salary for simple tasks. Moves conversation to Telegram. Crypto payment.

SCENARIO: "LOOK WHO DIED" (Facebook/Instagram Phishing)
Input Type: Social Media DM
Content: "OMG is this you in this video?? Look what happened, I can't believe it! [LINK]"
Why Scam: Classic clickbait to steal login credentials. Vague, alarming text designed to trigger curiosity.

SCENARIO: CEO FRAUD (The "Boss" Scam)
Input Type: Email
From: "Dan The Boss" <ceo.private.office@protonmail.com>
Subject: Urgent Task
Content: "I am in a meeting with investors. I need you to process a wire transfer to this vendor immediately. Do not call me. Handle this discreetly."
Why Scam: Impersonation of authority. Demand for secrecy. Use of private email (@protonmail) instead of company email.

SCENARIO: ROMANCE SCAM / PIG BUTCHERING
Input Type: WhatsApp
Content: "Hi, I think I saved the wrong number, sorry! You look like a nice person in your photo. I am Alice, a fashion designer from Singapore. Do you invest in Ethereum?"
Why Scam: "Wrong number" excuse to start chat. Flattery. Pivot to Crypto/Investment.

SCENARIO: GRANDPARENT SCAM
Input Type: Phone Transcript / Text
Content: "Hi Grandpa, it's me. I'm in trouble. I got arrested in Mexico/Spain. Please don't tell mom. I need money for bail wired via Western Union."
Why Scam: Emergency involving family. Request for untraceable money (Western Union). Secrecy ("Don't tell mom").

SCENARIO: RENT SCAM
Input Type: Facebook Marketplace
Content: "I am currently abroad so I cannot show you the apartment in person. But if you send the deposit via TransferWise, I will mail you the keys."
Why Scam: Refusal to meet in person. Request for money before seeing the product.

CATEGORY: TECHNICAL SUPPORT & ALERTS (HIGH RISK)

SCENARIO: MICROSOFT VIRUS POPUP
Input Type: Browser Text
Content: "WINDOWS DEFENDER ALERT: Your PC is infected with Zeus Virus. Access is blocked. Call Microsoft Support immediately: +1-800-123-4567."
Why Scam: Microsoft never asks you to call a phone number via a browser popup. Fear mongering.

SCENARIO: FAKE ANTIVIRUS RENEWAL
Input Type: Email
From: "McAfee Support" <renewals-mcafee@gmail.com>
Content: "Your subscription has auto-renewed for $399. Call us to cancel."
Why Scam: Trying to panic user into calling a fake refund center.

CATEGORY: SAFE PATTERNS (DO NOT FLAG THESE)

SCENARIO: LEGIT MARKETING (Electric Castle)
Input Type: Newsletter
From: "Electric Castle" <news@ecfestival.com>
Content: "Our X-MAS Packs are LIVE! Get your tickets secured for €10. The lineup includes Massive Attack. Buy now or cry later. Unsubscribe here."
Why Safe:
- Verified domain (@ecfestival.com).
- Marketing hype ("Buy now!") is NOT a security threat.
- Standard unsubscribe link.

SCENARIO: LEGIT HR (Recruitment Process)
Input Type: Email
From: "HR Team" <recruiting@corporation.com>
Content: "Salut! Mulțumim pentru aplicare. Te rugăm să rezolvi task-ul tehnic. Ai link-ul de Google Drive aici: https://drive.google.com/file/d/xyz... Deadline: Luni, ora 14:00."
Why Safe:
- Context is a job application user started.
- Google Drive is standard for sharing tasks.
- Deadline is procedural, not a "threat".

SCENARIO: LEGIT BANK NOTIFICATION (Transactional)
Input Type: SMS / Push
Content: "BCR: Tranzactie autorizata 50 RON la Mega Image. Sold disponibil: 450 RON. Data: 12/12/2024."
Why Safe:
- Informational only.
- No link to click.
- No call to action ("Call us or else").

SCENARIO: LEGIT 2FA CODE
Input Type: SMS
Content: "Apple ID Code: 884923. Don't share this code with anyone."
Why Safe:
- Standard security procedure.
- Explicit warning to be safe.

SCENARIO: FRIENDLY CHAT (Casual)
Input Type: Text Message
Content: "Hey! The weather is super nice today. Do you want to go grab a beer later? I can pay for the Uber."
Why Safe:
- "Pay for Uber" is a friendly offer, not a scam.
- Casual tone.
- No suspicious links.

SCENARIO: WORK EMAIL (Internal)
Input Type: Email
From: "Project Manager" <pm@mycompany.com>
Content: "Hi team, please find the attached PDF for tomorrow's meeting. Review it by 9 AM."
Why Safe:
- Internal domain matches.
- Context is work-related.
- Attachment is expected.

SCENARIO: ORDER CONFIRMATION

Content: "Comanda ta #12345 a fost predata curierului. Vei primi un SMS in ziua livrarii."

SCENARIO: PASSWORD RESET (Requested)

Why Safe:

- Safe if triggered by the user.

Why Safe:
