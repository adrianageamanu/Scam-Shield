SCAM PATTERN: URGENT BANK ALERT
Subject: ACTION REQUIRED - Your account is locked
Body: Dear Customer, we detected unauthorized access attempts. For your security, your account is temporarily suspended. Click here [LINK] to verify your identity within 24 hours or your funds will be frozen.
Indicators: Urgency, Suspended Account, Link to verify.

SCAM PATTERN: THE ROMANCE SCAM / PIG BUTCHERING
Context: Stranger on WhatsApp/Instagram.
Script: "Hello, I think I have the wrong number but you look kind. I am [Name], a fashion designer/investor from [City]. Do you invest in crypto? I can teach you how to make 20% weekly returns."
Indicators: Flattery, Wrong number excuse, Crypto investment promise, moving conversation to WhatsApp.

SCAM PATTERN: CEO FRAUD / BUSINESS EMAIL COMPROMISE
Subject: Urgent Transfer Request
Body: "I am in a meeting and cannot talk. I need you to process a wire transfer to this vendor immediately. It is for the acquisition we discussed. Please handle this discreetly and right now."
Indicators: Impersonating Boss, Secrecy, Urgency, Wire Transfer.
